International Students

Requirements

As a general policy, a student applicant who complies with the admission documents and eligibility requirements may enroll.

Incoming College Freshman

Graduate of Philippine High School
  • Grade 12 Report Card
  • Passport bio page
  • Special Study Permit
  • NBI Clearance
Graduate of High School Abroad
  • Authenticated Grade 12 (SHS) Transcript of Records
  • Passport bio page
  • Notice of Acceptance
  • Notarized Affidavit of Support

Graduate Program Int'l Students

including transferees

From a Philippine School
  • CHED Endorsement * (for transfer students)
  • Transfer Credential
  • Original copy of grades or Official Transcript of Records
  • NBI Clearance
  • I-Card / Latest VISA stamp

* Documents for CHED Endorsement:

submitted 2 months prior to enrollment

  • Letter of Intent to Enroll
  • Transfer credential
  • Original copy of grades
  • Passport bio page
  • I-Card / Latest VISA stamp
From a School Abroad
  • Authenticated Transcript of Records
  • Authenticated Police Clearance
  • Passport bio page
  • Notarized Affidavit of Support
  • Notarized of Acceptance (NOA)

Procedures

Step 1

Accomplish the Online UC Admission Form

Step 2

A confirmation message will be sent via SMS or email.

Step 3

After admission, courses shall be encoded. Pre-registration Form of enrolled courses assessed fees and payment instructions shall be sent via email.

Step 4

Pay fees through the following payment options.

Option 1

Cashier's Office, UC Main Campus (Monday to Saturday, 8:00AM to 5:00PM)

Option 2

Banks (BDO/ METROBANK/ PNB/ RCBC/ UNIONBANK)

*No account numbers needed

  • Online Banking thru Bank's Mobile App or Bank's Website, use BILLS PAYMENT option
  • Over the Counter using PAYMENT SLIP or BILLS COLLECTION SLIP

    Biller/Company's Name: University of the Cordilleras

    Subscriber's Name: 'Student's Full Name'

    Subscriber's Number: 'Student's Temporary ID Number'

Send proof of payments indicating the student's name to hei.payments@uc-bcf.edu.ph.

Note:

The official registration form, student ID and Official UC e-mail account shall be sent via email.
