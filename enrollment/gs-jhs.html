Grade School and Junior High School

Requirements

As a general policy, a student applicant who complies with the admission documents and eligibility requirements may enroll.

Incoming Kinder

  • Must be 5 years old by October 31 of the current school year
  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate

Incoming Grade 7

  • Report Card (SF 9)
  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • Recent 2 pcs 2x2 ID Picture
  • Latest Income Tax Return or Certificate of Tax Exempt or Certification of Unemployment of Parent/Legal Guardian

Transferees

  • Report Card (SF 9)
  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • ESC Certificate (for JHS ESC Recipients)

Procedures

Step 1

Accomplish the Online UC Admission Form

Step 2

A confirmation message will be sent via SMS or email.

Step 3

Pay fees through the following payment options.

Option 1

Cashier's Office, UC Main Campus (Monday to Saturday, 8:00AM to 5:00PM)

Option 2

Banks (BDO/ METROBANK/ PNB/ RCBC/ UNIONBANK)

*No account numbers needed

  • Online Banking thru Bank's Mobile App or Bank's Website, use BILLS PAYMENT option
  • Over the Counter using PAYMENT SLIP or BILLS COLLECTION SLIP

    Biller/Company's Name: University of the Cordilleras

    Subscriber's Name: 'Student's Full Name'

    Subscriber's Number: 'Student's Temporary ID Number'

Send proof of payments indicating the student's name to gsjhs.payments@uc-bcf.edu.ph.

Step 4

The official registration form, student ID and Official UC e-mail account shall be sent via email.
