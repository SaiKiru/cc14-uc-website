    Research Agenda

    The University of the Cordilleras Research Agenda, with the cross-cutting priority disciplinal thrusts, leans towards academic and social development by means of effective, sustainable, relevant, and quality knowledge transfer. Research is maximized through cross-college cooperation, external linkage and collaboration, and multidisciplinary/interdisciplinary approach to come up with advancing the contributions of the university in the nation's social, cultural, health, environmental, educational, and economic development. The enrichment of the research culture in the university by utilizing the capability and productivity of its faculty members and students is greatly emphasized to attain the institution's vision, mission, and objectives.

    Institute for Innovative Learning and International Cooperation

    Internationalization has been one of the top priorities of UC. Internationalization is embedded in all the key results area in the University's current strategic plan. For instance, in the key results area on education, the university is working towards the enhancement of the global skills and competence of its faculty through mobilization programs in the form of faculty exchange, acceptance of visiting professors and experts, strengthening of research collaboration programs with foreign institutions, and attendance in international training and conferences, among others.

    International partnership agreements and external grants have enabled the University to send faculty members, staff, and students to various academic programs, training, conferences, and events in countries like the United States, Canada, Thailand, Singapore, South Korea, China, and Malaysia. The said academic mobility initiatives have been very beneficial to the UC community. Upon their return to UC, the grantees were able to share their experiences with their colleagues, conduct impactful projects and programs, and initiate policy reforms that are inspired by the new learning experiences and exposure they have acquired abroad.

Sports Development Office

Background

One of the most enduring and successful extracurricular programs of the University is its athletic varsity program. Fielding top-caliber teams for inter-school sports meet and even international competitions; the University of the Cordilleras (UC) occupies one of the higher tiers for athletic programs in the city of Baguio and in the Northern Luzon. Sportsmen bearing the school colors have instilled a deep sense of pride not only for the students and the entire UC community as evidenced by many championships it had won in many team and individual sports. But this pride also resonates throughout the city and the Northern Luzon region because of the UC varsity's success in more international events, culminating in the victories of UC Varsity players during the Beijing Wushu Olympics 2008 competitions.

Vision

To be a model program excelling in academics and athletics.

Mission

To provide quality programs that cultivate excellence in academics, sport and in life.

In cognizance of the University’s mission to develop and deliver relevant professional curricula and developmental opportunities in a learner-centered environment, SDO’s primary commitment is to provide opportunities for student-athletes to fully develop their academic and athletic potentials to achieve great success and become productive citizen of the country.

To realize this, the Sports Development Office shall:

  1. promote the programs of the University of the Cordilleras as an institution that transforms individuals into a productive citizen of the nation;
  2. recruit and attract gifted students (particularly indigent students) to best represent the University both in the classroom and on the athletic field from the regional up to the international level of competitions;
  3. provide each UC student-athlete with the appropriate academic resources to enable them to pursue and achieve their college degree and prepare them for their career after graduation;
  4. support the welfare and development of the student-athlete personally, professionally and socially through an integrated system of care and support through providing relevant programs and services-standard trainings, team building, recognition ceremonies, victory party, Seminars and workshops etc;
  5. foster a respectful culture that utilizes and celebrates those differences by embracing the diversity of UC student-athletes, coaches and administrators;
  6. expand and enhance UC athletic facilities and equipment that serve UC student-athletes and coaches’ developmental needs while creating unforgettable experiences with the UC Jaguars supporters and with the nearby communities;
  7. foster partnership with former UC student-athletes and recognize their accomplishments and contributions to the University’s Sports Development programs; and
  8. foster camaraderie and harmony between the University, UC student athletes, citizens of Baguio and nearby communities.
